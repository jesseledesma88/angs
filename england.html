England

Discovering the Charm of England

England is a beautiful tapestry of rolling green countryside, dramatic coastlines, and centuries of history packed into bustling cities and quaint villages. Whether you are sipping afternoon tea in a cozy Cotswolds café, exploring the historic streets of Bath, or taking in the fast-paced energy of London, there is a distinct charm here that calls you back time and time again.

During my travels, I found that taking the train is one of the best ways to see the varying landscapes. The locals are welcoming, the pub food is surprisingly hearty and delicious, and the history is palpable at every corner.

Ang's Smart Travel Tips

  • Weather: Always pack layers and a compact umbrella. The weather can change multiple times in a single day!
  • Transport: Book intercity train tickets (like National Rail) well in advance to save money. Get an Oyster card for London.
  • Etiquette: Never skip the queue (line). Queueing is practically a national sport in England!

Towns & Cities I've Explored

London

A vibrant mix of historic grandeur and modern edge. Must-dos include the British Museum, walking along the Thames, and exploring Borough Market.

Bath

Famous for its Roman-built baths and stunning honey-colored Georgian architecture. It feels like stepping straight into a Jane Austen novel.
