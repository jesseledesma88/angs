Meet Your Guide

Hi! I’m Saw Lin, and I believe that the best chapters of our lives are written with a passport in hand. After decades of exploring the world, I have learned that the secret to a great trip isn’t just where you go, but how prepared you feel.

I established Ang’s Savvy Travel Guide to help seasoned travelers like you reduce the stress and get straight to the magic. Whether you’re cruising down the River Seine, chasing the Northern Lights or finding the best Nyonya food in a hidden alleyway, I’m here to make sure you have the right gear, the right plan, and the confidence to say "yes" to the next adventure.

These are the guiding principles that shape my travels and the community I aim to build.

  • Community Connect a community of seasoned travellers through shared cultural and travel experiences.
